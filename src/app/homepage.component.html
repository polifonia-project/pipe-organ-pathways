Deep Viewpoints contains short scripts that guide you through looking at and responding to artworks. Many of the scripts have been developed by citizen curators, providing a new perspective on the IMMA collection.

As well as taking part in the scripts you can also view the responses of others.

Scripts are organised into themes. Select a theme to begin. Tap on the artwork to start the script.
